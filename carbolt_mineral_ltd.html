Carbolt Mineral Ltd

Cobalt Mineral LTD has been mining coal and other minerals in Australia in the past 10 years. Their main quarry is in Olympic Dam, south of Australia. They mainly sell iron ore and some uranium ores too nuclear power plants and iron ore extractors. However, in recent months Cobalt Mineral LTD had consistent decline in sales by 5% as there was an increase in the number of competitor’s quarry in the local area form 20 quarries to 60 quarries. The board of directors has been deciding in expanding the corporation away from the primary market as the global demands have been shifting away from the primary market. For such expansion plans, the board of directors have been deciding to open the business to the public and become a public limited corporation.
